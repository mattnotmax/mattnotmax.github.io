About bit_of_hex

  2018-04-14  Comments

My name is Matt, and work in Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR) based in Melbourne, Australia. This blog, bit_of_hex, allows me to share my research and analysis.

I’m contactable on email at matt@bitofhex.com or via Twitter at @mattnotmax.

While ever effort has been made to ensure the accuracy of this blog, readers should never (for this or any other blog) rely on its content without their own up-to-date research and verification. My research has been done on configurations of software and hardware that may differ from your own. As such, testing may be incomplete or different to your circumstances. Where available I can make available raw testing data to interested persons. Please contact me directly if you require any data-sets.

All of the content on this website and social media are my personal opinions, research and analysis. No content, links or endorsements are related in any way to my employer.
